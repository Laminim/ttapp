Hearing Test

Quick hearing check with an audiogram at the end
How it works

Use headphones and sit in a quiet room.

After you press start, you will first get a short volume check.

For each frequency, the tone starts louder and slowly gets softer. Press Stop as soon as you can no longer hear it.

At the end, you will see a simple audiogram. This test is only a rough guide and does not replace a professional hearing test.
